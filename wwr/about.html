History

Indus and Zanskar river meet here and locally knows as "Sangam Point". Tourist can have a bird's eye view from Leh-Srinagar highway. For a close look, there is a nice downhill road to confluence.
This point is known as the highest river rafting point in the word which is elevated 11600 feet from sea level.
